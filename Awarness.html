Common Questions

How can I report illegal waste dumping in my neighborhood? expand_more
You can use our 'Report' tab in the navigation bar. Simply snap a photo, pin the location, and our municipal team will be notified immediately for cleanup.
What are the new recycling categories starting next month? expand_more
Starting next month, we're introducing e-waste, soft plastics, and organic composting as new categories. Look for the color-coded bins being rolled out across all neighborhoods.
How is the city measuring the CO2 reduction? expand_more
The city uses a combination of IoT sensors, satellite data, and third-party audits to calculate CO2 reduction. Reports are published quarterly and available on our Dashboard.
